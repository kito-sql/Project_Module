WE RUN IN DJERB

At Djerba Running Club, we take part in many exciting events across Tunisia and also organize our own activities here on the island.

Our mission is to offer our members new challenges, unforgettable memories, and the joy of sharing sport together.

ABOUT US

We are more than a running club, we are a family.

United by our love for sport, health, and the beautiful island of Djerba, we run together, support each other, and grow stronger with every step.

Founded in 2023, the Djerba Running Club was created by a group of passionate runners who wanted to share their love for running and promote a healthy lifestyle on the island of Djerba.

We started as a small group of friends meeting every weekend, today, we are a big family united by one goal: run together, stay healthy, and spread positive energy.
