ABOUT US

We are more than a running club — we are a family.

United by our love for sport, health, and the beautiful island of Djerba.

Founded in 2023, the Djerba Running Club began as a small group of passionate runners.

Today, we are a big family united by one goal: run together, stay healthy, and spread positive energy.
