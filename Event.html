WE RUN IN DJERB

At Djerba Running Club, we take part in many exciting events across Tunisia and the region. We pride ourselves on representing Djerba with pride wherever we run. These events bring us together as a community of runners, create unforgettable memories, and the joy of chasing sport together.

INTERNAL EVENTS

These are the events organized by our club on the island of Djerba. Most of these events take place every week. Below are some examples of our activities:

Sunday Run

This run happens every Sunday in a different location.

It's a welcome to everyone, beginners, experienced runners and families.

Tour de l'île

Our club organizes a full tour around the island of Djerba twice a year.

It's a day of sport, friendship, and discovering the beauty of unique landscapes of Djerba.

Camping Trip

A camping night filled with running, food, music and unforgettable moments.

A perfect opportunity to build team spirit and connect with nature.

Lablabi Run

One of our most beloved internal runs.

A friendly and energetic race full of smiles and motivation, ending with eating lablabi all together.

Night Trail - Galala

A special night trail in Galala, with headlamps, adventure, and exciting challenges for all participants.

Borg Castille Run

A challenging 15 km run in the beautiful historical area of Borg Castille.

Combining sport, history, and nature in one includes breathtaking views.
